How PaceBudget Works

1. The Goal: Fixing "Front-Loading"

The primary cause of budget failure is "front-loading": spending too much at the start of the month. PaceBudget treats your monthly budget as a daily wage. If your budget is $300, you have $10 today. You must wait to "accrue" funds.

2. Intended Use: Discretionary Only

Do not include fixed bills like Rent here. This is for elastic expenses (dining, hobbies, gadgets).

3. The "Look-Ahead" Balance

The dollar amount on a day is your Safe-to-Spend Balance. It looks ahead at future scheduled items and reserves cash for them automatically.

4. Smart Scheduling & The Queue

Use the Planning Queue to prioritize items. The AI calculates the earliest date you can afford an item without breaking your budget.
